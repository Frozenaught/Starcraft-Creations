Flexible Prints

These parts are printed with flexible materials. Flexible filaments are made of Thermoplastic Elastomers (TPE) which are a blend of hard plastic and rubber.

PLA, ABS & PETG

These materials are the industry standard for prototyping. ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) has a long history in the 3D printing world.

Fiber Printing

With our high resolution printer you can have a detailed prototype. Carbon fiber filaments use tiny fibers that are infused into a base material to improve the properties of that material.

Metal Printing

We have a wide variety of material for any application. Metal filled filaments contain very fine metal powder such as Copper, Bronze, Brass, and Stainless Steel.

Nylon

Nylon (a.k.a. Polyamide) is a popular material in the plastics industry, known for its toughness and flexibility. We have a wide variety of choices.

custom printing

Our engineers will design and print your parts to the highest quality. We have over 30 materials to choose from and 50 printers running 24/7.
